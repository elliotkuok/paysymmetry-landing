About Us

Our goal is to help you negotiate for a better salary and recognize pay discrimination by giving you the tools to anonymously share salary information with your coworkers and friends. We were inspired by this video about why sharing your salary with coworkers is so important and hopefully, you are too.

PaySymmetry is always free to use, but if it’s helped you earn more money we hope you will consider paying it forward with a small donation. We will never share your salary with anyone outside of your group, including advertisers.

You can learn more about us on our F.A.Q.’s page.
